# Trading Guide

Master the art of trading gold-backed digital assets on GOX with this comprehensive guide.

## Understanding Gold Tokens

### PAXG (Pax Gold)

**Issuer**: Paxos Trust Company  
**Backing**: 1 token = 1 troy ounce (31.1g) physical gold  
**Storage**: London Good Delivery bars in Brink's vaults  
**Chain**: Ethereum (ERC-20)  
**Redeemable**: Yes (minimum 1 oz)  
**Audited**: Monthly attestations by third-party auditors

**Key Features**:
- Lowest fees for redemption
- Highest liquidity
- Regulated by NYDFS
- Insured storage

### XAUT (Tether Gold)

**Issuer**: TG Commodities Limited (Tether)  
**Backing**: 1 token = 1 troy ounce physical gold  
**Storage**: Swiss vaults (LBMA-approved)  
**Chain**: Ethereum, Tron (multi-chain)  
**Redeemable**: Yes (minimum 50 oz)  
**Audited**: Quarterly attestations

**Key Features**:
- Multi-chain support
- Lower premiums
- Tether ecosystem integration
- Swiss storage jurisdiction

### gXAU (GOX Gold)

**Status**: Coming Q1 2026  
**Backing**: 1 token = 1 troy ounce physical gold  
**Chain**: Multi-chain (Ethereum, Arbitrum, Polygon)  
**Redeemable**: Yes (minimum 1 oz)

**Planned Features**:
- Lowest trading fees
- Native GOX ecosystem
- Governance rights
- Staking rewards

## Trading Strategies

### 1. Buy and Hold

**Goal**: Long-term gold exposure  
**Time Horizon**: 6-24 months  
**Risk**: Low to Medium

**Strategy**:
```plaintext
1. Buy PAXG/XAUT during market dips
2. Hold through market cycles
3. Monitor gold spot price trends
4. Sell during price peaks
```

**Best For**: Investors seeking gold hedge against inflation

### 2. Premium Arbitrage

**Goal**: Profit from token premium differences  
**Time Horizon**: 1-7 days  
**Risk**: Low

**Strategy**:
```plaintext
1. Monitor PAXG vs XAUT premium to spot gold
2. Buy token trading at discount
3. Sell token trading at premium
4. Repeat when spreads widen
```

**Example**:
```plaintext
Gold Spot:  $2,645.00
PAXG:       $2,646.50 (+0.06% premium)
XAUT:       $2,644.20 (-0.03% discount)

Action: Buy XAUT, Sell PAXG
Profit: 0.09% spread ($2.30 per oz)
```

### 3. Swing Trading

**Goal**: Capture short-term price movements  
**Time Horizon**: 1-14 days  
**Risk**: Medium

**Strategy**:
```plaintext
1. Use technical analysis (RSI, MACD, MA)
2. Buy on oversold conditions (RSI < 30)
3. Sell on overbought conditions (RSI > 70)
4. Set stop-loss at -3%
5. Take profit at +5-10%
```

**Technical Indicators**:
- **Moving Averages**: 20-day, 50-day, 200-day
- **RSI**: Relative Strength Index
- **Volume**: Confirm breakouts
- **Support/Resistance**: Key price levels

### 4. Dollar-Cost Averaging (DCA)

**Goal**: Reduce timing risk  
**Time Horizon**: 12-36 months  
**Risk**: Low

**Strategy**:
```plaintext
1. Set monthly investment amount ($500-5000)
2. Buy PAXG/XAUT on same day each month
3. Ignore short-term price movements
4. Accumulate over time
5. Rebalance annually
```

**Example Schedule**:
```plaintext
Day 1 of month: Buy $1,000 PAXG
Average cost:   $2,628/oz over 12 months
vs. Lump sum:   $2,645/oz (higher entry)
```

## Order Types

### Market Order

**Execution**: Immediate  
**Price**: Current market price  
**Best For**: Fast execution, liquid pairs

```typescript
// Market Order Example
{
  type: 'market',
  side: 'buy',
  amount: 1.0, // 1 PAXG
  pair: 'PAXG/USDT'
}
```

**Pros**:
- Instant execution
- Guaranteed fill
- Simple to use

**Cons**:
- Price slippage on large orders
- No price control
- Higher costs in volatile markets

### Limit Order (Coming Soon)

**Execution**: When price reaches target  
**Price**: Your specified price  
**Best For**: Patient traders, better prices

```typescript
// Limit Order Example
{
  type: 'limit',
  side: 'buy',
  amount: 1.0,
  price: 2640.00, // Target price
  pair: 'PAXG/USDT'
}
```

**Pros**:
- Price control
- Better execution
- Lower costs

**Cons**:
- May not fill
- Requires patience
- Price monitoring needed

## Advanced Trading

### Slippage Settings

Control maximum price deviation:

- **0.1%**: Low slippage, may fail in volatile markets
- **0.5%**: Standard setting for most trades
- **1.0%**: Higher tolerance for large orders
- **2.0%**: Maximum for urgent trades only

### Gas Optimization

Save on transaction costs:

```plaintext
Network Comparison:
- Ethereum: $5-50 per trade (peak: $100+)
- Arbitrum: $0.50-5 per trade
- Polygon: $0.01-0.50 per trade

Recommendation:
- Small trades (<$1000): Use Arbitrum/Polygon
- Large trades (>$10000): Ethereum is acceptable
```

### MEV Protection

Protect against front-running:

1. **Use Private RPC**: Submit transactions privately
2. **Flashbots**: MEV-protected transactions
3. **Limit Orders**: Avoid market orders during volatility
4. **Split Orders**: Break large trades into smaller chunks

## Risk Management

### Position Sizing

Never risk more than you can afford to lose:

```plaintext
Portfolio Allocation:
- Conservative: 5-10% in gold tokens
- Moderate: 10-20% in gold tokens
- Aggressive: 20-30% in gold tokens

- Max 5% of portfolio per trade
- Use stop-losses at -3% to -5%
```

## Trading Psychology

### Emotional Discipline

1. **Don't FOMO**: Avoid chasing pumps
2. **Cut Losses**: Accept small losses early
3. **Take Profits**: Lock in gains systematically
4. **Stay Calm**: Don't panic during dips
5. **Be Patient**: Wait for setups

### Common Mistakes

❌ **Over-trading**: Too many unnecessary trades  
✅ **Solution**: Set daily/weekly trade limits

❌ **Revenge Trading**: Trying to recover losses  
✅ **Solution**: Take breaks after losses

❌ **Ignoring Fees**: Not accounting for gas + fees  
✅ **Solution**: Calculate total costs before trading

❌ **No Plan**: Trading without strategy  
✅ **Solution**: Write trading plan and follow it
