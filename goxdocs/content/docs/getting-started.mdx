# Getting Started with GOX

This guide will help you start trading gold-backed digital assets on GOX in just a few minutes.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you'll need:
- A Web3 wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, Coinbase Wallet)
- Some ETH for gas fees
- Funds to trade (USDT, USDC, or ETH)

## Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

### Using MetaMask

1. Visit [gox.exchange](https://gox.exchange)
2. Click **"Connect Wallet"** in the top right
3. Select **MetaMask** from the wallet options
4. Approve the connection request in MetaMask
5. Your wallet address will appear in the navbar

### Using WalletConnect

1. Click **"Connect Wallet"**
2. Select **WalletConnect**
3. Scan the QR code with your mobile wallet
4. Approve the connection

### Supported Wallets

- MetaMask
- WalletConnect
- Coinbase Wallet
- Trust Wallet
- Rainbow Wallet
- Ledger (via MetaMask)

## Step 2: Add Funds

You can fund your wallet with:

### Option A: Buy Crypto with Fiat
1. Use the built-in fiat on-ramp
2. Click **"Buy Crypto"**
3. Enter amount and payment method
4. Complete KYC verification (if required)

### Option B: Transfer from Exchange
1. Withdraw USDT, USDC, or ETH from your CEX
2. Use **Ethereum mainnet** or **Arbitrum**
3. Copy your wallet address from GOX
4. Initiate withdrawal from exchange
5. Wait for confirmation (5-30 minutes)

## Step 3: Your First Trade

### Swap Tokens

Navigate to the **Swap** page:

```
gox.exchange/swap
```

1. **Select Input Token**: Choose USDT, USDC, or ETH
2. **Enter Amount**: Type how much you want to swap
3. **Select Output Token**: Choose PAXG or XAUT
4. **Review Details**:
   - Exchange rate
   - Price impact
   - Gas fees
   - Estimated output
5. **Click "Swap"**
6. **Confirm Transaction** in your wallet
7. Wait for confirmation (usually 1-3 minutes)

### Example Swap

```plaintext
Input:  1,000 USDT
Output: 0.378 PAXG (~$1,000 worth)
Rate:   1 PAXG = $2,645.50
Fee:    0.3% ($3.00)
Gas:    ~$5-10 (depends on network)
```

## Trading Tips

### Best Practices

1. **Start Small**: Begin with a small test transaction
2. **Check Gas Fees**: Trade during low-traffic hours (UTC 2-8 AM)
3. **Set Slippage**: Use 0.5-1% slippage for stable trades
4. **Monitor Premium**: Check PAXG/XAUT premium to gold spot
5. **Use Limit Orders**: Coming soon for better price execution

### Understanding Premiums

Gold tokens may trade at a premium/discount to spot gold:

```plaintext
Gold Spot:  $2,645.00/oz
PAXG Price: $2,646.12 (+0.04% premium)
XAUT Price: $2,644.85 (-0.01% discount)
```

Premium factors:
- Token liquidity
- Market demand
- Redemption fees
- Storage costs

### Gas Optimization

Save on gas fees:

1. **Batch Transactions**: Combine multiple swaps
2. **Use Arbitrum**: 10x cheaper than Ethereum
3. **Off-Peak Hours**: Lower gas during weekends
4. **Set Gas Limit**: Manual gas settings in MetaMask
5. **Use Fast**: Avoid "Slow" gas options

## Common Issues

### Transaction Failed

**Cause**: Insufficient gas or slippage too low

**Solution**:
1. Increase gas limit by 20%
2. Set slippage to 1-2%
3. Try again with fresh gas estimate

### Token Not Showing

**Cause**: Token not imported to wallet

**Solution**:
1. Manually add token contract address
2. Check correct network (Ethereum/Arbitrum)
3. Refresh wallet

### High Price Impact

**Cause**: Large trade size or low liquidity

**Solution**:
1. Split into smaller trades
2. Use different pool
3. Wait for better liquidity
