# Staking & Farming

Earn passive income on your gold-backed tokens through GOX's staking and liquidity farming programs.

## Overview

GOX offers multiple ways to earn yields:

- **Single-Asset Staking**: Stake PAXG, XAUT, or gXAU to earn rewards
- **Liquidity Farming**: Provide liquidity to pools and earn trading fees + rewards
- **Lock Staking**: Higher APY for longer lock periods
- **Flexible Staking**: Unstake anytime with lower APY

## Single-Asset Staking

### How It Works

1. Deposit your gold tokens into staking contract
2. Earn rewards in same token or GOX governance token
3. Rewards accrue daily and compound automatically
4. Withdraw anytime (flexible) or after lock period

### Impermanent Loss

⚠️ **Important**: Liquidity providers face impermanent loss risk.

**What is IL?**

When token prices diverge, your LP position may be worth less than holding tokens separately.

**Example**:

```plaintext
Initial:
- Add 1 gXAU @ $2,645 + 2,645 USDT
- Total Value: $5,290

Scenario 1: gXAU +20% to $3,174
- Your LP: 0.913 gXAU + 2,898 USDT = $5,796
- Just HODLing: 1 gXAU + 2,645 USDT = $5,819
- IL: -$23 (-0.4%)

Scenario 2: gXAU +100% to $5,290
- Your LP: 0.707 gXAU + 3,741 USDT = $7,482
- Just HODLing: 1 gXAU + 2,645 USDT = $7,935
- IL: -$453 (-5.7%)
```

**Mitigation**:
- Farm stable pairs (USDT-USDC): no IL
- Choose pairs with similar price movement (gXAU-PAXG)
- Earn fees + rewards to offset IL
- Short time horizons reduce IL risk

- Multi-signature controls
- Insurance fund: $2M coverage

### Market Risk

- Gold price volatility

- Liquidation risk (if borrowing)

### Lock-Up Risk

- Funds locked for selected period
- No early withdrawal
- Opportunity cost if prices spike
- Consider flexible pools for short-term

## Reward Calculations

### Single Staking Formula

```typescript
dailyReward = (stakedAmount * APY) / 365
annualReward = stakedAmount * APY

// Example: 2 gXAU @ 52.8% APY
dailyReward = (2 * 0.528) / 365 = 0.00289 gXAU
annualReward = 2 * 0.528 = 1.056 gXAU
```

### LP Farming Formula

```typescript
// Trading Fee APY
feeAPY = (dailyVolume * feeRate * 365) / TVL

// Farm Reward APY
farmAPY = (dailyGOXRewards * GOXPrice * 365) / TVL

// Total APY
totalAPY = feeAPY + farmAPY

// Example: gXAU-USDT Pool
feeAPY = ($980,000 * 0.003 * 365) / $8,750,000 = 12.2%
farmAPY = (450 * $15 * 365) / $8,750,000 = 28.1%
totalAPY = 40.3%
```

## Tax Implications

⚠️ **Disclaimer**: Consult a tax professional. This is not tax advice.

### US Tax Treatment

**Staking Rewards**:
- Taxed as ordinary income when received
- Basis = FMV at receipt time
- Capital gains on appreciation when sold

**LP Fees**:
- Trading fees may be ordinary income
- Adding/removing liquidity can be taxable events
- Impermanent loss may not be deductible until realized

**Record Keeping**:
- Track all stake/unstake transactions
- Record reward claim dates and values
- Log LP additions/removals
- Use crypto tax software (Koinly, CoinTracker)

## FAQ

### Can I unstake early?

**Flexible pools**: Yes, anytime with no penalty  
**Lock pools**: No, must wait until lock period ends

### When are rewards distributed?

Rewards accrue every block (~12 seconds) and are:
- Auto-compounded (if enabled)
- Claimable anytime
- Distributed continuously, not in batches

### What happens to rewards during unstaking?

Rewards are automatically:
1. Calculated up to unstake time
2. Claimed to your wallet
3. No rewards lost

### Can I add to an existing stake?

Yes:
- Flexible pools: Add anytime
- Lock pools: Starts new lock period from addition date

### How do I maximize APY?

1. Choose longest lock period you're comfortable with
2. Enable auto-compounding
3. Add to position during market dips
4. Combine staking + farming for dual yields
5. Move to higher APY pools when available
